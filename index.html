Minesweeper

Objective of the game

The objective of the game is to clear a minefield without detonating any!

What can you do?

You will be able to assign columns and rows with a maximum of 19x19.

Place the number of bombs you want on the board

Rules

1. The boxes have a number that indicates the number of mines in the surrounding cells

2. If you discover a square with a mine, the game is lost.

3.If a box with a 0 is found, it indicates that none of the neighboring cells has a mine and will be discovered automatically.

4.If you do not customize the game the board will be 8x8 and 8 bombs will be assigned.
